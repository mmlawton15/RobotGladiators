A Bit of Background

While she isn't everyone's favorite artist, its hard to deny she has talent. Whether you watch her live performances on the TV or in person, she remains one of the few well-known artists to posess raw, un auto-tuned talent. I was lucky enough to witness her in concert during her Reputation tour, and the electricity that pulses through the crowd when she sings is unmatched. Her obsessive fans are obsessive for obvious reasons. Through thick and thin publicity, she has managed to maintain a monolithic fan base of manic fans, ranging from all different backgrounds. She has seen her fare share of drama, and instead of letting it ruin her career, she used it to propel her to even greater heights, as seen in her Reputation album.
